5501 Abercorn Street is a rare chance to buy scale, corridor identity, and repositioning control on one of Savannah's most established commercial spines at a basis that should be compelling to owner-users, medical-adjacent operators, and value-add investors. The most compelling reason to acquire the property is the combination of approximately 48,943 square feet of existing improvements and 3.6 acres of land control at a $5,000,000 asking price, which implies an approximate $102 per square foot basis before buyer diligence adjustments.

The timing is favorable because Savannah continues to benefit from port-linked employment, healthcare demand, higher education, tourism, population growth, and durable infill retail and service demand. Abercorn Street remains a practical connector between Midtown, the medical district, established residential neighborhoods, and Southside commercial activity.

The value-creation path is repositioning. A buyer can stabilize the building for owner occupancy, subdivide for professional office or medical users, activate showroom and service-retail demand, or hold the site as a longer-range redevelopment position subject to zoning and approvals.

The ideal buyer is an owner-user seeking a Savannah flagship, a private investor with leasing capability, a medical or education-adjacent operator, or a redevelopment-minded group that values land control on a corridor where replacement cost and entitlement timing are difficult to replicate.
